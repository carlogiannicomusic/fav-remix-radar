[01] The app

Built to audition fast.

  • Tap to play

    One tap previews any track — a 30-second clip with a scrubber to jump back and forth.

  • Ranked for momentum

    24h / 7-day / month growth across four platforms, so you catch a track while it's rising.

  • Like & build playlists

    Heart what you'll flip, drop it into a renamable “to remix” list, jump straight to Spotify.

  • Artists & TikTok sounds

    Not just songs — who's breaking out, and the sounds being used most right now.

[02] How it works
01

Scan

Every morning we pull the top charts on each platform, across three windows — 24h, 7 days, a month.

02

Rank

Tracks are scored for remix potential: momentum, freshness and cross-platform pickup.

03

Deliver

The radar updates instantly, and the day's top picks land in a private Telegram drop.

04

[04] Install

On your Home Screen in 2 taps.

No app store, no download, no account. Remix Radar is a web app — add it once and it opens full-screen like a native app, with its own icon.

iPhone / iPad
  1. Open this page in Safari.
  2. Tap Share (the square with the up-arrow).
  3. Tap Add to Home Screen → Add.
Launches full-screen · own icon
Android
  1. Open this page in Chrome.
  2. Tap menu ⋮.
  3. Tap Install app / Add to Home screen.
Installs as a PWA
Desktop

Just open it in any browser — or click the install icon in the address bar (Chrome / Edge) to get a desktop window.
